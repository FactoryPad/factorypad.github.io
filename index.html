OVERVIEW

What Prod does

Prod combines workflow organization, agentic AI assistance, and publishing tools into one product experience. The platform can help generate content ideas, prepare drafts, support scheduling logic, coordinate review flows, and execute authorized publishing actions for connected social media accounts.

✦

Content Workflow Support

Organize content from idea to draft to approval using a centralized dashboard-like experience built for recurring publishing pipelines.

⚙

Agentic AI Assistance

Use AI to help with brainstorming, formatting, operational recommendations, and workflow handoffs while preserving user control over final actions.

🔒

Authorized Account Actions

Connected account permissions are used to support approved tasks such as publishing, scheduling, metadata preparation, and related app functionality.

📈

Publishing Operations

Support structured publishing workflows for short-form and social content with room for operational review, queue management, and ongoing refinement.
